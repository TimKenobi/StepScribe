Features

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

Built for people who take their recovery seriously. No social feeds, no streaks, no guilt — just a quiet place to do the work.

✍️

Rich Journal Editor

Beautiful rich text with bold, italic, headings, lists, and blockquotes. Voice dictation when you'd rather talk. Attach photos and files.

🧠

AI That Remembers

Your AI sponsor extracts insights from every conversation and entry. It learns your struggles, strengths, triggers, and milestones — and never forgets.

📋

12 Guided Prompts

Morning reflection, step work, resentment inventory, gratitude list, amends preparation, and more. Structure when you need it.

🌤️

Inner Weather

Track your mood with 12 poetic states — from "Storm" to "Clear Skies." See your energy trends over time without pressure.

🛡️

Heroes & Wisdom

24 built-in heroes — saints, writers, philosophers. Daily rotating quotes woven naturally into your AI conversations.

📖

Export Your Story

Export your journal as a beautifully formatted Markdown book. JSON backup and restore. Your words are always yours.

AI Sponsor

A companion who actually listens

StepScribe's AI doesn't just respond to prompts — it builds a living memory of who you are. Your struggles, your breakthroughs, your patterns. Every conversation makes it wiser.

  • ✓ Persistent memory across all sessions
  • ✓ Auto-compaction keeps context focused
  • ✓ Adapts to your faith tradition
  • ✓ You control what it knows — view, edit, or delete any memory
💬
Inner Weather

No streaks. No pressure.
Just showing up.

Track your inner weather with poetic mood states that feel human, not clinical. See your energy trends over the last two weeks. Every day you write is a victory — we never make you feel bad for missing one.

  • ✓ 12 weather-inspired mood states
  • ✓ Energy level tracking (1–10)
  • ✓ Calendar view with clickable days
  • ✓ Mood trend visualization
